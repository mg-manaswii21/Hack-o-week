Three Trips. Countless Memories.

From mountains to movie cities to magical castles: By Manaswi Ghode

Manali – Summer Camp Adventure

With The Himalayan Camp CAC Allrounders

My summer in Manali was filled with adventure, bonfires, trekking trails, and breathtaking Himalayan sunrises. Staying with the CAC Allrounders team made the experience unforgettable — filled with teamwork, laughter and growth.

Hyderabad – Family Trip of 10

Resort Stay • Treks • Ramoji Film City

Traveling with 10 family members was chaos in the best way. We relaxed at a beautiful resort, went on scenic treks, and explored the cinematic world of Ramoji Film City. It was bonding, laughter and pure joy.

Hong Kong Disneyland – 30 Member Family Trip

Magic • Rides • Fireworks

Visiting Hong Kong Disneyland with 30 family members felt like living inside a dream. From thrilling rides to magical parades and fireworks lighting the sky, it was childhood nostalgia brought to life.
